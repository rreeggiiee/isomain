Iso (real name Li Zhao Yu) is a Duelist agent in VALORANT hailing from Chongqing, China. Introduced in Episode 7: Act III, he is a fixer and lethal hitman who reconfigures ambient radianite energy into tactical utility and bulletproof shields. Because his kit entirely focuses on gunplay and raw aim rather than movement mechanics, he is a premier pick for players confident in winning isolated firefights.
